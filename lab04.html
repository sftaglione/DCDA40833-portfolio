Tableau Exploratory Data Analysis

Sophia Taglione | DCDA 40833 | Spring 2026

Overview

In this lab, I used Tableau Public to conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA) on a dataset I selected. The goal was not simply to create a visually appealing chart, but to identify meaningful patterns, breaks in patterns, and unexpected outliers within the data. By experimenting with different metrics, dimensions, and filters, I explored how visual structure influences interpretation and insight. This lab emphasizes analytical reasoning, honest prediction, and curiosity rather than surface-level design.
